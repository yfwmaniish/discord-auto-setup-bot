🤖 DISCORD BOT TOKEN CREATION GUIDE
=====================================

Since you're already logged in to Discord Developer Portal, follow these EXACT steps:

📍 STEP 1: CREATE APPLICATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
👀 LOOK FOR: Big blue button that says "New Application"
✅ CLICK: "New Application"
✅ TYPE: "Auto Setup Bot" (or any name you want)
✅ CLICK: "Create"

📍 STEP 2: GO TO BOT SECTION  
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
👀 LOOK FOR: Left sidebar menu
✅ CLICK: "Bot" (in the left menu)
✅ CLICK: "Add Bot" button
✅ CLICK: "Yes, do it!" when asked

📍 STEP 3: ENABLE INTENTS (IMPORTANT!)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
👀 LOOK FOR: "Privileged Gateway Intents" section
✅ TURN ON: "Message Content Intent" (toggle switch)
✅ TURN ON: "Server Members Intent" (toggle switch)

📍 STEP 4: GET YOUR TOKEN
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
👀 LOOK FOR: "Token" section
✅ CLICK: "Copy" button
✅ PASTE: The token here in our chat

📍 STEP 5: GET INVITE LINK
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
✅ CLICK: "OAuth2" (in left menu)
✅ CLICK: "URL Generator" 
✅ CHECK: "bot" under Scopes
✅ CHECK: These permissions:
   - Send Messages
   - Manage Messages
   - Manage Channels
   - Manage Roles
   - Manage Webhooks
   - Read Message History
✅ COPY: The generated URL at the bottom

🎯 WHAT TO DO NEXT:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. Just paste your bot token here
2. I'll automatically configure everything
3. We'll test your bot
4. You'll have a working Discord bot!
